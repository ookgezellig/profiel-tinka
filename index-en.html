Tinka den Arend

Complete Profile Research date: December 1, 2025 Status: Public information
"I believe it's important that PPF APG continues to add value for participants, retirees, and for APG and affiliated employers."
Get in touch

Tinka den Arend is an experienced professional in the Dutch pension sector who plays a key role in the historic transition to the new pension system under the Future of Pensions Act (Wtp).

As Head of Policy and Public Affairs at APG — the largest pension administrator in the Netherlands with approximately €616 billion in assets under management — she advises the Executive Board and fund clients on external developments, legislation, and strategic positioning. Additionally, since 2019 she has served as employer chair of the PPF APG board (Staff Pension Fund APG), where she oversees investment policy and the fund's long-term strategy.

Her career is characterized by a broad background in the public sector: she previously worked at the Dutch House of Representatives, PwC as a consultant, the Ministry of Social Affairs and Employment as a policy officer, and the National Institute for Social Insurance (LISV). This diverse experience gives her unique expertise at the intersection of pension legislation, political processes, and implementation.

Award-Winning Leadership

Under her chairmanship, PPF APG was among the three "frontrunners" that were the first Dutch pension funds to successfully transition to the new pension system. This led to the first pension payments under the new system in February 2025.
